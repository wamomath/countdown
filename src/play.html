Welcome to the WAMT Countdown Round! Twelve students will compete in a single-elimination bracket. Players are seeded based on their written round rankings. The top four written round scorers will receive a bye in the first round, meaning that they automatically move to the second round. The remaining competitors will face off in the first round, with 5th against 12th, 6th against 11th, and so on. Questions will be read aloud by the moderator and displayed on both contestants' screens and the large screen up front. Either student may buzz in at any time, even before the moderator finishes reading. After buzzing in, the student will have 3 seconds to begin their answer or lose their chance to answer that question. If the student answers incorrectly, the other contestant will have the remainder of the question read to them and will have the remainder of the time limit to answer.
The first student to answer three questions correctly wins the match and advances to the next round. Players have 45 seconds to answer each question, including the time the moderator spends reading the question. If no one buzzes within that time, nobody gets the point.
